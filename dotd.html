Dia de
los Muertos

Annual Day of the Dead Festival

For 30 years, the traditions of Dia de los Muertos — Day of the Dead — have been honored and celebrated by community members of San Rafael, Marin County and beyond. The celebration comes from the ancient wisdom of the indigenous people of Mezzo and South America that through the years has been open to many spiritual influences of this ever-developing world and changing universe we live in.

The multicultural community of the Canal Neighborhood hosts an annual Dia de los Muertos celebration and welcomes residents and visitors to the City of San Rafael to share in a grateful acknowledgement of the continuing ebb and flow of existence.

The Multicultural Center of Marin is a co-organizer of the event and contributes to the goal of honoring the cultural assets of immigrant communities and sharing the cultural legacy of Latin American cultures with Marin residents.
